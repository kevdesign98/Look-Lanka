Why LookLanka?

Sri Lanka is a paradise, but navigating "tourist prices" and persistent taxi drivers can be stressful. We provide real data, honest prices, and community alerts to ensure you enjoy only the best of the island.

Women's Travel Guide

Solo Female Safety Hub

Sri Lanka is a magical destination for women traveling alone. We've collected practical tips from our community to help you feel safe at all times.

Dress with Respect

Dressing modestly (shoulders and knees covered) isn't just for temples. It helps you blend in and avoid unwanted attention in markets or on public transport.

Pro Tip: Always carry a sarong.

Safe Commute

Always use PickMe for evening Tuk-Tuks. Every ride is GPS-tracked and you can share the trip with a friend.

Sisterhood

On public buses, try to sit next to other women or families. It's the local norm and discourages troublemakers.
