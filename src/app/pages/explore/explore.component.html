Cultural Triangle

Sigiriya Rock

Rise above the jungle. This ancient monolith isn't just a rock — it's a testament to 5th-century genius. Skip the expensive "official guides" at the entrance.

Fair Price

$30 / person

Ignore "helpers" near the stairs. They will charge you 5,000 LKR for a "push".

Central Highlands

Ella Village

Where the tea clouds touch the mountains. Ella is the soul of the island's nature. Best experienced by foot or local train.

Fair Price

Free (Hiking)

Train tickets to Kandy: Buy 2nd class (unreserved) for 600 LKR. Don't pay 5k for resellers.

Hill Country

Kandy: Temple of the Tooth

"Where history meets spirituality in the misty hills."

Honest Tip

The evening ceremony (Theva) is loud and crowded. For a peaceful visit, go at 5:30 AM. Dress code is strict: shoulders and knees must be covered (white clothes preferred but not mandatory).

Southern Coast
Southern Coast

Mirissa Beach

"Golden sunsets and giants of the oceans."

Fair Price Check

Whale watching should cost around $40–$60. If it's too cheap, the boat is likely overcrowded and lacks safety gear. Look for Raja & the Whales for ethical sightings.
